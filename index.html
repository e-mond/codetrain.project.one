Welcome to the Object Identification Game

Get ready for a fun and educational adventure where you'll put your object identification skills to the test. In this game, you'll be presented with images of various objects, and your mission is to correctly identify them from a list of options.

Here's how to play:

  1. Examine the displayed image carefully.
  2. Select the correct object name from the provided options.
  3. Click the answer button to confirm your choice.
  4. If you need assistance, use the "Hint" button to highlight two options.
  5. Keep an eye on the timer; you have 10 seconds for each question.
  6. Your score will be displayed at the top right corner.
